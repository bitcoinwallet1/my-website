Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/10/2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application Satguards: Bitcoin Wallet (the “Application”).

1. Data Collection

  • The Application itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data or wallet information.
  • All wallet keys and data remain on your device and are never transmitted to us.
  • The only information we may receive is if you voluntarily contact us (e.g., by email).

2. Use of Information

If you contact us, we may use the information you provide only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Provide customer support.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.

4. Data Retention

  • App usage: No data collected, no retention.
  • Support inquiries: Retained only as long as needed to respond, then securely deleted.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under GDPR, CCPA, LGPD), you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your information.
  • Restrict or object to processing.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at support@satguards.com. Requests will be handled within the legally required timeframe.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has provided information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We take commercially reasonable measures to protect any information you voluntarily provide. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Law Enforcement

We do not maintain user databases. If legally required, we can only provide support emails voluntarily sent to us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Please review periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • Email: support@satguards.com
